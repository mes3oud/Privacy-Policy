MudFlow — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

MudFlow ("the app", "we", "our") is a drilling fluids engineering toolkit developed for professional use in field and office environments. This policy explains what data MudFlow accesses, how it is used, and what choices you have.

Summary: MudFlow is offline-first. By default, no data leaves your device. Cloud Backup is entirely optional and, when enabled, stores your data only in your own Google Drive — never on MudFlow's own servers.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

MudFlow does not collect, transmit, or have any access to:

  • Your well geometry, mud properties, calculation inputs/outputs, or any engineering data you enter
  • Your location, contacts, camera, or microphone
  • Analytics or tracking identifiers used for advertising

All calculators, Well Profiles, and Calculation History are stored locally on your device using local app storage. This data is never transmitted to us and is not visible to us at any time.

2. Optional Cloud Backup (Google Drive)

MudFlow offers an optional Cloud Backup & Sync feature. This feature is off by default and only activates if you choose to sign in with your Google account.

What happens if you enable it

  • You sign in using Google Sign-In. MudFlow requests access only to a narrow Google Drive permission scope (drive.file) — this means MudFlow can only see, create, edit, and delete files that MudFlow itself creates. MudFlow cannot see, browse, or access any other files or folders in your Google Drive.
  • MudFlow creates a single file named mudflow_backup.json in your own Google Drive, visible to you at any time.
  • This file contains your Well Profiles, Calculation History, and Report Branding settings (company name, contact line, engineer name — logos are not included in this backup).
  • Backups happen automatically after changes, and can also be triggered manually.

Where this data lives

This data is stored exclusively inside your own Google Drive account, governed by Google's Privacy Policy. MudFlow does not operate any backend server or database that stores this data. We have no access to your Google Drive contents, your backup file, or your Google account credentials at any time.

Disabling or deleting backup

You can sign out of Cloud Backup at any time from within the app. You can also delete the mudflow_backup.json file directly from your Google Drive at any time — MudFlow will simply create a new one on the next backup if you choose to re-enable the feature.

3. In-App Purchases

MudFlow offers optional premium features (such as Report Branding and Cloud Backup) via Google Play's in-app billing system. All payment processing, billing information, and transaction handling is managed entirely by Google Play — MudFlow does not receive, store, or process your payment card details, billing address, or any financial information.

We only receive confirmation from Google Play as to whether a purchase was completed, in order to unlock the relevant feature on your device.

4. Data Shared With Third Parties

MudFlow does not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. The only third-party services MudFlow interacts with are:

  • Google Play Billing — for processing optional in-app purchases
  • Google Drive API — only if you explicitly enable Cloud Backup, and only within the narrow file-scoped permission described above

5. Report Exports (PDF / Excel)

Kill Sheets and other reports you export as PDF or Excel files are generated and saved locally on your device (or shared directly by you via your device's share function). MudFlow does not upload or transmit exported reports anywhere.

6. Company or Third-Party Well Data

If you use MudFlow to record data related to wells or operations owned by your employer or a third party, you are responsible for ensuring your use of Cloud Backup (if enabled) complies with your employer's or client's data handling policies. Since Cloud Backup stores data only in your personal Google Drive account, we recommend confirming with your employer whether this is an appropriate storage location for operationally sensitive data before enabling this feature.

7. Children's Privacy

MudFlow is a professional engineering tool intended for use by drilling fluids engineers and industry professionals. It is not directed at, marketed to, or intended for use by children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app's features. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of MudFlow after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
